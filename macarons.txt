Could you tell me more about this Macarons?
A macaron is a sweet meringue-based confection made with egg white, icing sugar, granulated sugar, almond meal, and often food colouring.
What is a macaron?
A macaron is a sweet meringue-based confection made with egg white, icing sugar, granulated sugar, almond meal, and often food colouring.
What are the ingredients in the Macarons?
The Macarons are made with egg white, icing sugar, granulated sugar, almond meal, food colouring.
What flavors of macarons do you offer?
We offer a variety of flavors, including classic options like vanilla, chocolate, and raspberry, as well as seasonal flavors like lavender and pistachio.
Are your macarons gluten-free?
Yes, our macarons are gluten-free as they are made with almond flour.
Do you have any vegan macarons?
Unfortunately, our macarons contain egg whites, so they are not vegan. However, we have other vegan options in our bakery.
Can I mix and match flavors in a box of macarons?
Of course! You can create your own assortment by choosing from our available flavors.
Are the macarons made fresh daily?
Yes, our macarons are made fresh every day to ensure the best quality.
Do you offer any special or seasonal macarons?
Absolutely! We have seasonal macarons that change throughout the year, featuring flavors inspired by the current season.
What is the shelf life of your macarons?
Our macarons are best enjoyed within a few days, but if stored properly in the refrigerator, they can last up to a week.
Are your macarons nut-free?
No, our macarons contain almond flour, so they are not suitable for those with nut allergies
Can you recommend a flavor that's a customer favorite?
Our most popular flavor is the salted caramel macaron. Customers love its perfect balance of sweetness and saltiness.
